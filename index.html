Quick Exercises

1. Stretch Breaks: Take short stretching breaks every hour to improve flexibility.

2. 20-Minute Walk: Daily short walks can improve physical fitness and overall well-being.

3. Breathing Exercises: Find a moment for deep breathing to relax your mind and body.

Healthy Diet

1. Fruits and Vegetables: Consume a variety of fruits and vegetables to provide vitamins and fiber to your body.

2. Protein: Include sources of protein such as poultry, fish, eggs, or legumes.

3. Avoid Processed Foods: Limit the consumption of highly processed products with artificial additives.

Healthy Sleep

1. Create a Sleep Routine: Go to bed and wake up at consistent times to regulate your biological clock.

2. Comfortable Environment: Create a peaceful sleeping space with the right temperature and darkness.

3. Avoid Caffeine Before Bed: Limit caffeine intake in the evening to avoid disrupting sleep.
